About me

I am 20 years old and started studying programming when I was nineteen. Altough it has become a more and more attractive career and hobbie, I have other passions to which I am fully devoted to, be that: Art. Besides being a developer, I am also a constantly learning oil painter. I enjoy passing my time brushing strokes and creating new shapes, people, views, animals, everything you could imagine. Furthermore, it is in my plans to follow the career of Art History next year, since I have come to the realisation that that is the career that wraps up everything I long to know about: well, obviously, Art.
History. And I'm not only talking about the life-story of the painter, but also about the sociological and cultural aspects that revolved around his or her work. Every painter's work is undeniably directly related to society's status. And learning not only about the story of a painter, but also the world's history at the same time, sounds marvellous to me.
Philosophy. Philosophy is theoretical from beginning to end, whereas art is sensuous and imaginal. Philosophical thought reflects its subject-matter in concepts, in categories; art is characterised, on the other hand, by emotional and imaginal reflection and by transformation of reality. Astonishment. A concept viewed by philosophy as the true nature of all beings, is a concepts that is chained to art. When you look into a painting and it surprises you, you are reminded that you are still human. Think about kids, as pure as they are, they get surprised about everythings because they do not take anything for granted, the world is a masterpiece to them, a whole new scenery to be discovered. That is art. And this text, long as it may be, is a part of who I am.
